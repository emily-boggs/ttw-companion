# Product Context

_What the app does, who it's for, and what problems it solves._

---

## Problem Statement

The Two-Week Wait (TWW) is one of the most emotionally challenging parts of trying to conceive. People obsessively symptom-spot, feel isolated, and have no dedicated supportive tool. Existing period/fertility trackers are clinical, overwhelming, and not emotionally attuned to this specific phase.

---

## Target User

- Anyone TTC (trying to conceive) who is currently in their TWW
- Likely 25–40, digitally native, seeking emotional support alongside information
- May be on their first cycle or their twentieth — the anxiety doesn't diminish

---

## Core Value Proposition

**"Your gentle companion through the longest two weeks."**

A warm, supportive app that:
1. Helps you understand what's happening in your body each day
2. Gives you a healthy outlet for symptom tracking (instead of obsessive Googling)
3. Provides evidence-based test timing guidance
4. Connects you with others going through the same thing
5. Meets you with encouragement, not clinical detachment

---

## Key User Flows

### 1. Start a TWW
User enters ovulation date → app generates personalized 14-day timeline starting from DPO 1.

### 2. Daily Check-in
User opens "Today" → sees what's happening in their body → logs symptoms → gets encouragement.

### 3. Browse Timeline
User views all 14 days → taps a day → sees body guide + logged symptoms for that day.

### 4. Community
User reads posts from others in TWW → hearts supportive messages → posts their own update → can delete their own posts.

### 5. Should I Test?
On each day, user sees whether testing is recommended and the reliability level of results at that DPO.

---

## Competitive Landscape

| App | Gap TWW Companion Fills |
|---|---|
| Flo / Clue | Full cycle trackers — TWW is buried, not emotionally supportive |
| Premom | Focused on ovulation prediction, not post-ovulation support |
| Fertility Friend | Data-heavy, clinical UI, overwhelming for casual users |
| Reddit/Forums | Supportive community but no structured daily guidance |

---

## Success Metrics (MVP)

- User can complete a full 14-day TWW flow
- All pages render correctly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Community interaction works (post, heart, reply, delete)
- Deployed and shareable via link
