# Product Context

_Why this project exists and what value it delivers._

---

## Problem Statement

The two-week wait (TWW) — the time between ovulation and a reliable pregnancy test — is one of the most anxiety-filled experiences in the fertility journey. People are left googling symptoms at 2am, doom-scrolling Reddit, and obsessing over every twinge with zero structured support.

Existing fertility apps (Flo, Clue, Ovia) are **cycle trackers** — they require months of data and don't address the emotional reality of the TWW. No dedicated, warm, supportive tool exists for *this specific moment*.

---

## Target User

- Women / people with cycles aged 20–38 actively trying to conceive
- On their phone, often at night, anxious, seeking reassurance and information
- Moderate tech comfort — familiar with apps, not necessarily tech-savvy
- Emotional state: hopeful but anxious — needs warmth, not clinical coldness

---

## User Experience Goals

| Goal | How |
|---|---|
| **Immediate value** | Enter ovulation date → personalized timeline in under 60 seconds |
| **No friction** | No account, no onboarding walls, no data collection |
| **Emotionally supportive** | Warm micro-copy, soft visuals, encouragement throughout |
| **Informative without overwhelm** | Day-by-day body guide — what's potentially happening at each DPO |
| **Actionable** | Clear "Should I Test?" indicator so users stop guessing |

---

## What Success Looks Like

A user opens the app on their phone, enters their ovulation date, and immediately receives a personalized timeline, daily body info, and a place to log how they're feeling — in under 60 seconds, with no account required.
